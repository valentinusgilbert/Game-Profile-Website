NOKA 3 can already be pre-installed on Google Play Store or App Store and players can start playing on June 11, 2022, at 10.00 AM GMT

GAME UPDATED
May 26, 2022

ESPEON UNLEASHES ITS POWER IN NOKA 3

Add some more psychic power to your team now that Espeon has arrived in NOKA 3. For a limited time, you can acquire Espeon’s NOKA license without spending Aeos gems through a special in-game Espeon event starting at midnight on May 23, 2022. Earn points by logging in, participating in and winning battles, and other in-game activities. You can then redeem Espeon’s NOKA license once you’ve earned 2,000 points in this event.

GAME UPDATED
May 17, 2022

DURALUDEON SHOWS ITS METAL IN NOKA 3

Spin out the competition in NOKA 3 with Duraludon, now available to join your team. This Ranged Attacker is ready to leverage light, throw claws, and rupture the very earth to claim victory. Duraludon’s basic attack is a fierce blast of light fired from its mouth, dealing damage to opposing Pokémon it hits.

GAME UPDATED
May 14, 2022

NOKA 3 GOES FULL-FURY FOR NOKAi DAY

NOKA Day happens just once a year on June 15, but fortunately, NOKA Day in NOKA 3 event will be celebrating your love of Heroes for over two weeks! From May 15 to May 29, 2022, Pokémon UNITE will be upping its game with special log-in bonuses and a thrilling new type of quick battle—full-fury battles.

GAME UPDATED
Dec 15, 2021

NOKA’S HOLIDAY FESTIVITIES WILL BE SNOW MUCH FUN

NOKA 3 is embracing the merriment of the season with holiday festivities running from December 15, 2021, to January 16, 2022. Players can get special log-in gifts such as reward boxes containing limited licenses and Trainer fashion items, earn special event rewards with the Illumination Challenge and Photo Challenge, and add two new NOKA licenses to their collection as Tsareena and Dragonite enter the fray.
